About the Game

Vietnam’s coasts and rivers face rising plastic pollution from single-use items, inadequate sorting, and storm-driven runoff. In CoastGuard, each match links a real problem to a practical solution used by communities, NGOs, and local initiatives.

  • Plastic bag → Reusable tote
  • Styrofoam lunch box → Reusable lunch box
  • Single-use straw → Metal/bamboo straw
  • Ghost fishing nets → Net retrieval & recycling
  • Polluted canal → Community cleanup & booms
  • Trash in drains → Grate cleaning & awareness
  • Open burning → Sorting & proper collection
  • Festival litter → Green events & volunteers
